# Modality Pack: Generic Fallback

Use when HAYF does not yet have a deep specialist pack for the selected modality.

## Principles

- Preserve the user's selected modality without pretending deep sport expertise.
- Keep dose conservative and technique-focused.
- Prefer maintenance exposure, skill practice, or low-intensity conditioning until better evidence exists.
- Do not invent specialized interval prescriptions.

## Archetypes

- `generic_skill_practice`: technique or skill continuity, 20-60 minutes, low-to-moderate fatigue.
- `generic_easy_conditioning`: easy repeatable exposure, 20-60 minutes, low fatigue.
- `generic_support_session`: bounded support work, 20-45 minutes, low-to-moderate fatigue.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating an unsupported modality like generic cardio.
- Creating precise prescriptions without sport knowledge.
- Letting novelty crowd the primary goal.
