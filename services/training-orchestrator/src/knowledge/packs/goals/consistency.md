# Goal Pack: Consistency

- The goal is repeatability, not maximal adaptation in the first weeks.
- Minimum effective dose should be clear enough that a bad week still counts.
- Primary success signal: completed planned sessions or active training days.
- Use conservative progression: add reliability before adding intensity.
- Avoid complex splits, too many modalities, and frequent novelty.
- Good architecture usually has no phases; it uses review cadence instead.
