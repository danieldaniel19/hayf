# Goal Pack: Body Composition

- Training should protect lean mass, preserve useful performance, and support energy expenditure without excessive fatigue.
- Strength work is usually protected when the user wants visible athleticity, muscle definition, or strength retention.
- Endurance can support energy expenditure and health, but excessive intensity or volume can undermine recovery.
- Weight-loss goals need modest fatigue management; do not chase both maximal endurance performance and maximal hypertrophy at once.
- Good signals include strength continuity, training completion, body metric trend availability, and recovery caveats.
