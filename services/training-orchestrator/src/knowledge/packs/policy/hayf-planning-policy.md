# HAYF Planning Policy

Product-level coaching rules that constrain all specialists.

- Supabase is the product backend and source of truth. Graphs return artifacts and traces; Edge persists canonical rows.
- Planning packets may contain compact evidence summaries, never raw HealthKit samples or workout ledgers.
- The Training Architecture owns modality roles, tradeoffs, weekly budget, recovery envelope, and planner constraints.
- Specialist consultants advise inside the Training Architect's frame; they do not create dated workouts.
- The planner compiles the validated architecture into calendar workouts and must not reopen strategy decisions.
- Minimum viable week comes before optional training.
- Adherence and recoverability beat theoretical optimality for consistency and mixed-modality goals.
- Do not invent certainty when evidence is missing. Use conservative ranges and call out caveats.
- Off-menu modalities must not be introduced unless the architecture explicitly allows them.
- Week 1 is committed; week 2 is draft.
